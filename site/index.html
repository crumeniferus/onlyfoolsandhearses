Only Fools and Horses Stag-Do

The horse-drawn hearse was unhitched so that this bunch of likely lads could send off their comrade into married life. We managed to grab this quick snap mid-mayhem but if you want to see some high quality images of the Only Fools and Hearses three-wheeler, take a look at the gallery.

You could also hire a limousine or two from our partner company, R H Funeral Services. Put the name aside for a moment and imagine how spectacular it will look: two splendid limousines led from the front by the infamous yellow three-wheeler!

All the fleet is kept in top condition. Mostly. We don't want the Reliant Robin to look too good otherwise it would lose its authenticity!
